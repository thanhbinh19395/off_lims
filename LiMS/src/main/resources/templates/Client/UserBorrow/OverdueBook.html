Fines for very overdue items

We take the non-return of library items seriously and academic sanctions do apply. You will be prevented from re-enrolling in OFF courses if within 14 days of receiving the bill for replacement:

  • You fail to return an item to the library OFF
  • You fail to pay its replacement cost

OFF Library reserves the right to take further action if you leave the University with Library property.

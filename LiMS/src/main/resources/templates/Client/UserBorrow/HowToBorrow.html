Borrowing book

By borrowing from the Library you agree to comply with our borrowing rules.

At first you need contacts with librarian to register a account. You can register at 222 Lê Văn Sỹ Q.3 TPHCM

Phone : 0123 456 789 - Mrs Cô


When you not have an account, you still can see the list book are having in library.
You can click on the book to see the detail. But only when you login, you can be reserve books.


You can click Borrow button and fill your infomation to reserve that book. You will be received a code on email.


You need take the code for the librarian and go to storage to get that book. And take it to librarian to complete the book borrow process



Recalled loans

  • You are guaranteed a loan of at least 7 days on standard items
  • External and remote students are guaranteed a loan of at least 14 days
  • We will request the return of the item by email
  • Return the item by the new due date to avoid overdue loans

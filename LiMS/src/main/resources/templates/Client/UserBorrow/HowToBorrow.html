Method 1 - Offline

Go straigh to our library, located at 280 An Duong Vuong, District 5, HCM City


At the librarian desk. Register account for using library service.


After finished registration, go inside the library and find you book you want to borrow.


Return to librarian desk to make borrowing book procedure


You can check your borrowing status on the library website.


Method 2 - Online

Go to OFF Library website


Search the book you may want to borrow


If your book is Available, the book will be able borrowed. If it was borrowed, you can make a reservation. Click here to know how to reserve book

The result will display all the book have the keyword you enterred. Click Detail.


If book is available. The Borrow Now will be display. In the other hand, Reserve Now button will be display. Click Borrow Now in case.


You will be redirect to book cart. If you want to continue borrow, click Keep Borrowing. If not, click Borrow to sent borrowing request.


When borrowing request have been sent. Another people cannot borrow this book right now. You have to go to OFF Library to take that book in 1 DAY. If not, your request will be automatically cancelled. And others allows borrow that book.


  • You are guaranteed a loan of at least 7 days on standard book
  • Teacher are guaranteed a loan of at least 14 days
  • We will request the return of the book by email
  • Return the item by the new due date to avoid overdue loans

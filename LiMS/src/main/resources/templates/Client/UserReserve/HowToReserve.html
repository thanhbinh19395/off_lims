How to make a reservation?

You want to borrow that book but it has been borrowed by others. Don't worry, you can reserve and borrow this when it returned.

Books with red sign is borrowed. Click at detail of the book which you want to borrow





Watch the Book's detail and make sure that you want borrow it. And now you must click at the Reserve button.




A dialog will show up provides for you the information about the date the book was returned




When you click ok, your request will be sent to the librarian. You can check your reserve in tab 2 View Log. And remember come to library to borrow this book on appointment.
